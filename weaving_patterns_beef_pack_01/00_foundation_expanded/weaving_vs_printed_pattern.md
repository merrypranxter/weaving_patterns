# Weaving Vs Printed Pattern

A woven pattern is structural. A printed pattern is surface-applied.

A printed plaid sits on top of cloth. A woven plaid is made from colored thread intersections.

## Woven tells

- visible interlacing at close range
- color changes aligned with thread direction
- anisotropic highlights
- floats and shadows
- slight tension irregularity
- selvedge behavior

## Prompt warning

Never ask for a woven pattern if you mean a flat print. Ask for printed textile, screenprint, or surface design instead.
