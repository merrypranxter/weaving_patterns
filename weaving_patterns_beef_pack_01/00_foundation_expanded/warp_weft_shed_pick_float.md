# Warp / Weft / Shed / Pick / Float

## Warp

Lengthwise threads held under tension.

## Weft

Crosswise thread inserted through the shed.

## Shed

The opening formed when selected warp threads lift.

## Pick

One insertion of weft.

## Float

A thread crossing over multiple perpendicular threads before interlacing again.

Floats are where surface personality happens: shine, shadow, snag risk, softness, and stripe direction.
