# Anisotropic Highlights

## Surface behavior

This file covers directional shine along thread axes.

## Why it matters

Without material behavior, a woven shader becomes a checkerboard wearing a cheap Halloween costume. The thread must feel physically present.

## Shader controls

- normal perturbation
- directional roughness
- fiber noise scale
- occlusion in thread gaps
- edge softness
- tension displacement
- highlight direction

## Prompt language

Use: macro textile surface, visible strand fibers, directional thread sheen, tiny shadows between interlaced yarns, uneven handwoven tension.
