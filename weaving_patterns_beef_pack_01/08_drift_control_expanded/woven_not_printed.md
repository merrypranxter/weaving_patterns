# Woven Not Printed

## Problem

AI systems often flatten woven structures into surface graphics. That kills the whole point.

## Diagnostic checks

- Are warp and weft individually visible?
- Does the color follow thread direction?
- Are floats casting tiny shadows?
- Does the pattern obey a repeat or draft?
- Does light catch differently along each thread axis?

## Repair prompt

Render as actual interlaced yarns, with visible over-under structure, thread-level macro detail, directional highlights, fiber fuzz, and cloth tension irregularity. Do not render as a flat printed pattern.
